Our Gemstone Collection

Garnet

Garnet is a symbol of passion, energy, and commitment. This deep red gemstone is believed to bring strength, courage, and protection to its wearer, making it perfect for expressing love and devotion.

Purple Aqeeq

Purple Aqeeq (Agate) is revered for its spiritual significance and healing properties. This beautiful stone is believed to bring peace, balance, and protection, making it a cherished choice for meaningful jewelry.

Firoza (Turquoise)

Firoza, or Turquoise, is a stone of wisdom, protection, and good fortune. Its distinctive blue-green color has been treasured for centuries, symbolizing friendship, peace, and positive energy.

Zabarjad

Zabarjad (Peridot) is known as the stone of compassion and renewal. Its vibrant green hue represents growth, prosperity, and emotional healing, bringing clarity and positive transformation to its wearer.
